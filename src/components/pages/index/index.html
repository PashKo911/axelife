Class IIa Medical Device with Clinical Validation

An innovative, non-operator-dependent, clinically-validated medical device that predicts cardiovascular risk in seconds by measuring arterial stiffness.

A New Standard in Cardiovascular Prevention

The pOpmètre® revolutionizes cardiovascular prevention by measuring pulse wave velocity (PWV) and calculating ankle brachial index and central pressures.

Rapid and Patient-Friendly Diagnostic Insights

The advantage of the pOpmètre® is also to provide the patient with a clear data point : vascular age, a more motivating indicator to help patients adopt healthy lifestyles.

Efficient, Reliable and Non-Invasive Screening

Its high reproducibility, compact format, and easy use make the pOpmètre® an asset for all medical specialties.

Our Partners

Clinics and Hospitals

The pOpmètre® is an asset for both you and your patients: cardiovascular risk measurement and assessment can be carried out in just a few minutes, while respecting your patients' privacy.

General practitioners

With the pOpmètre®, introduce the measurement of arterial elasticity into the care pathway, thus promoting screening and prevention of cardiovascular risks.

Specialized physicians

The pOpmètre® assesses arterial stiffness, an essential biomarker for the prevention of stroke, heart attack and cognitive impairment.

Multidisciplinary health centers

The advantage of the pOpmètre® is also to provide important data to the patient so that they can visualize and become fully aware of their health status.

Chantal, 61 ans

Clinical case: Ischemic stroke, no hypertension, diabetes or cholesterol. Investigated after rigorous medical evaluation, including one-week ECG Holter and endoesophageal PWV of 20 m/s (equivalent to vascular age of 100 years).

Treatments: ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, aspirin and sports nutrition > antioxidants, coriander and pears, including garlic.

Results: Three years later, PWV is 12 m/s
